FAQ

How to add a new event to get reminder?

You can go to the Add New Event page through the Dashboard and fill the event details. After filling it, click add to calendar. Your event data will be added to the calendar and you will be able to get reminders via sms and email.

How to add a new bill to get reminder?

You can go to the Add New Bill page through the Dashboard and fill the bill details. After filling it, click add to calendar. Your bill data will be added to the calendar and you will be able to get reminders via sms and email.

What can you get through the global event page?

According to your country and living area, we are offering you details of all the events that can be relevent to you. If you wish to, you can search them and add them to your calendar to get reminders for them also.

How to ask more questions?

If you have any more issues regarding the site, you can go to the Contact Us page and ask any question.

How to view your remider list?

You can go to My Profile page and view all the bill and event remiders that added before. You can edit or remove them from there also. If you want to add more, click on the Add button and then you will be redirected to the relevant pages.

What are the services you can get from our site?

You can get reminders for the events and bills. You can search for global events and add them to your calendar. You can get notifications after adding them to calendar to manage your time efficiently.
